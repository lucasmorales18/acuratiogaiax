# Legally Binding Act

## 1. Introduction

This Legally Binding Act (“Act”) sets forth the legal framework, obligations, and rights applicable to all participants (“Participants”) engaging in the [Your Dataspace Name] (“Dataspace”). By participating in the Dataspace, each Participant agrees to comply with the terms and conditions outlined herein.

## 2. Purpose

The purpose of this Act is to ensure that all data sharing, processing, and service provision within the Dataspace are conducted in accordance with applicable laws, regulations, and the Gaia-X Policy Rules.

## 3. Scope

This Act applies to all Participants, including but not limited to data providers, data consumers, service providers, and intermediaries operating within the Dataspace.

## 4. Legal Compliance

Participants must comply with all applicable laws and regulations, including but not limited to:
- Data protection and privacy laws (e.g., GDPR)
- Intellectual property rights
- Competition and antitrust laws
- Export control regulations

## 5. Obligations of Participants

Participants agree to:
- Act in good faith and with due diligence
- Ensure the accuracy and lawfulness of data shared or processed
- Respect the rights of other Participants
- Implement appropriate technical and organizational measures for data security

## 6. Liability

Each Participant is liable for its own actions and omissions. The Dataspace Operator is not liable for any damages arising from the actions of Participants, except in cases of gross negligence or willful misconduct.

## 7. Dispute Resolution

Disputes arising from or in connection with this Act shall be resolved amicably between the parties. If no resolution is reached, disputes may be submitted to mediation or arbitration in accordance with [Jurisdiction/Law].

## 8. Amendments

This Act may be amended by the Dataspace Operator. Participants will be notified of any changes in advance.

## 9. Governing Law

This Act shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of [Jurisdiction].

## 10. Acceptance

By participating in the Dataspace, each Participant acknowledges and accepts the terms of this Legally Binding Act.
