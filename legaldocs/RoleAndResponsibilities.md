# Role and Responsibilities

## Purpose

To define the roles and responsibilities of all parties involved in the Service Offering.

## Policy

- The Provider must clearly document the roles and responsibilities of all parties, including customers, users, and third parties.
- Responsibilities must cover areas such as data protection, security, incident response, and compliance.
- All parties must be informed of their roles and responsibilities prior to service engagement.

## Review

Roles and responsibilities must be reviewed and updated as necessary, especially when there are changes in personnel or service scope.
