# Government Investigation Management

## Purpose

To define procedures for handling government investigation requests, including legal review, informing cloud customers, and limiting access to or disclosure of Customer Data.

## Policy

- All government requests for information must be reviewed by legal counsel.
- Customers must be informed of such requests unless prohibited by law.
- Disclosure of Customer Data must be limited to the minimum required by law.

## Review

Procedures must be reviewed and updated as necessary to comply with legal requirements.
