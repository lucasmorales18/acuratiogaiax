# Change and Configuration Management

## Purpose

To ensure that all changes and configuration actions to the information systems are managed and controlled to maintain the security and integrity of the delivered cloud service.

## Policy

- All changes to information systems must be documented, reviewed, and approved prior to implementation.
- Configuration management processes must be established to track and control system configurations.
- Changes must be tested and validated to minimize the risk of security incidents or service disruptions.
- Emergency changes must be documented and reviewed post-implementation.

## Responsibilities

- The Provider is responsible for maintaining change and configuration management records.
- All personnel involved in system changes must follow established procedures.

## Review

This policy shall be reviewed and updated regularly to ensure ongoing effectiveness.
