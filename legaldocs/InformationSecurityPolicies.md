# Information Security Policies

## Purpose

To provide a global information security policy, derived into policies and procedures regarding security requirements and to support business requirements.

## Policy

- A comprehensive information security governance framework must be established, defining security objectives, scope, roles, responsibilities, and compliance requirements.
- Derived policies must address access control, data classification, incident response, backup and recovery, acceptable use, password management, and encryption.
- Security procedures must cover onboarding/offboarding, vulnerability management, change management, audits, and risk assessments.
- Policies must support business requirements, customer data protection, service availability, and regulatory compliance.

## Review

Policies and procedures must be reviewed, updated, and communicated regularly.
