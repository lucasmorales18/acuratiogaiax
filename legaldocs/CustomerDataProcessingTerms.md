# Customer Data Processing Terms

## Purpose

To specify the rights and rules for using the service and any Customer Data contained within it.

## Policy

- The Provider may process Customer Data only as necessary to deliver the agreed services.
- Data processing must comply with applicable data protection laws and contractual terms.
- The Provider must implement appropriate security measures to protect Customer Data.
- Customers retain ownership of their data and may request its return or deletion at any time.

## Review

These terms must be reviewed and updated in line with changes to services or legal requirements.
