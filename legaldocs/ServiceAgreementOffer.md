# Service Agreement Offer

## Purpose

To provide an EU law-governed contract template with GDPR-specific clauses defining data controller/processor roles, lawful basis for processing, data retention periods, and European jurisdiction for disputes.

## Policy

- The Service Agreement must clearly define the roles and responsibilities of all parties regarding data protection.
- Lawful bases for data processing must be specified.
- Data retention periods and deletion procedures must be documented.
- Dispute resolution must be governed by European law and jurisdiction.

## Review

The Service Agreement template must be reviewed and updated as necessary.
